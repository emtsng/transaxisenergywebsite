Corporate Supply

Over 50 million litres of diesel and gasoline supplied to construction and infrastructure companies.

Transaxis maintains dedicated bulk supply relationships with major construction, rail and infrastructure contractors operating across Nigeria — backed by a growing internal tanker fleet and structured supply partnerships with Dangote Petroleum Refinery, NNPC Trading and other credible sources.

  • Scheduled and emergency bulk deliveries
  • Dedicated account management for corporate clients
  • Nationwide delivery via an internally managed fleet
