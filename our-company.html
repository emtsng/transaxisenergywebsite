Company Overview

Reliable energy delivery, built on strong partnerships

Transaxis Energy Limited is a fully indigenous energy and infrastructure company operating across Nigeria's midstream and downstream oil and gas value chain. We specialise in the marketing, distribution, supply and logistics of refined petroleum products, delivering quality energy solutions that support industries, institutions, projects and communities across the country.

Incorporated in Nigeria in 2019, Transaxis Energy has rapidly evolved into a trusted energy partner with a vibrant and expanding business footprint. We currently service both retail and corporate clients through comprehensive bulk supply operations and a growing network of retail outlets.

Our strength lies in our strategic partnerships, supplier relationships and strong professional leadership, coupled with an unwavering commitment to operational efficiency, safety and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To rank foremost among Nigeria's leading energy and petroleum products suppliers, known for reliability, innovation and sustainable service delivery.

Our Mission

To provide reliable energy solutions that meet the needs of industries and consumers, while continuously expanding our market presence and maintaining the highest standards of integrity, efficiency and safety.
