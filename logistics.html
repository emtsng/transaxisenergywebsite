Logistics & Haulage

An internally managed tanker fleet, equipped with safety and tracking systems, ensuring efficient, secure and on-time delivery of petroleum products across Nigeria.

Overview

Logistics built for reliability

Our modern logistics division guarantees efficient and secure transportation of petroleum products. We operate a fleet of 13 tanker trucks equipped with safety and tracking systems, ensuring on-time deliveries across Nigeria. By managing our logistics internally, we eliminate third-party inefficiencies and ensure reliability even under volatile market conditions.

Every delivery is planned around depot coordination, route safety, and real-time visibility — giving our corporate and retail clients confidence in every consignment.

Technology

Building toward full fleet visibility

Transaxis is progressively integrating route telematics and tracking technology into its logistics operations as part of a broader ERP, Automatic Tank Gauging (ATG) and point-of-sale integration programme — improving transparency and delivery confidence for our clients.

  • Vehicle tracking for delivery visibility
  • Depot-to-destination coordination
  • Reduced dependency on third-party haulage
