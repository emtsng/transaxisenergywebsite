Industries We Serve

From heavy construction to public sector institutions, Transaxis delivers reliable energy supply tailored to the operating realities of every sector we serve.

Construction

Bulk AGO supply for earthworks, civil works and project-site power generation.

Rail & Infrastructure

Sustained diesel supply for rail construction corridors and infrastructure megaprojects.

Manufacturing

Fuel and industrial chemicals supporting production lines and factory operations.

Mining

Reliable bulk fuel delivery to remote mining sites and heavy equipment fleets.

Power Generation

AGO and HFO supply for captive power plants and backup generation.

Government & Public Sector

Structured fuel supply arrangements for government fleets, agencies and institutions.

Transport & Logistics

Fleet fuel supply and lubricants for haulage companies and transport operators.

Agriculture

Diesel supply and lubricants for farm machinery, irrigation and agro-processing.

Telecommunications

Dependable fuel supply for telecom towers, base stations and backup power systems.
