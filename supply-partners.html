Our Key Supply Partners

Structured relationships with Nigeria's leading refiners and traders strengthen our supply resilience, product availability, pricing competitiveness and nationwide delivery capability.

Dangote Petroleum Refinery & Petrochemicals FZE

Transaxis Energy holds an official Distributorship Appointment for Domestic Sales with Dangote Petroleum Refinery and Petrochemicals FZE, granting us access to locally refined petroleum products under a structured and transparent distribution framework. This partnership enhances our supply chain resilience and positions us to serve our customers reliably.

NNPC Trading Limited

Through our collaboration with NNPC Trading, we access a steady supply of refined petroleum products for both retail and bulk clients. This relationship underscores our commitment to working with the most credible upstream and midstream players in the industry.

MRS Oil Nigeria Plc

Our partnership with MRS Oil Nigeria Plc allows us to maintain product diversity and quality assurance across multiple depots. MRS provides a key supply channel that supports our bulk and corporate supply operations nationwide.

A.A Rano Nigeria Limited & Rano Upstream Limited

As part of our growing portfolio of private sector partnerships, A.A Rano Nigeria Limited and its upstream subsidiary, Rano Upstream Limited, provide us with flexible supply options and operational synergy in the trading and distribution of refined petroleum products. This relationship supports our bulk deliveries and aligns with our shared vision of sustainable growth in Nigeria's downstream sector.
