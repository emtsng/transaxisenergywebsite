Our Journey

Milestones since 2019

2019

Transaxis Energy Limited is incorporated in Nigeria and begins retail and bulk petroleum product operations.

2022 – 2023

New management commences a structured turnaround, strengthening governance, compliance and station-level operations.

2023 – 2024

Construction and management of a 360,000-litre diesel and gasoline tank farm; fleet expanded to 11 tanker trucks; formal distributorship secured with Dangote Petroleum Refinery & Petrochemicals FZE.

2024 – 2025

Nationwide retail stations converted to 100% solar-hybrid power; Transaxis Logistics and Transaxis Drilling Services established as new subsidiaries.

2025 – Onward

Advancing development of a 5.67 MMSCFD CNG Mother Station and five CNG Daughter Stations, alongside continued fleet and depot expansion.
