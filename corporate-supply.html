Corporate & Bulk Fuel Supply

Dependable, large-volume AGO and PMS supply for construction, infrastructure, manufacturing and institutional clients — backed by structured supply partnerships and an internally managed delivery fleet.

Overview

Fuel supply that keeps your operations running

Transaxis Energy is engaged in the wholesale distribution and bulk supply of refined petroleum products, including Automotive Gas Oil (AGO/Diesel), Premium Motor Spirit (PMS/Petrol), Dual Purpose Kerosene (DPK) and Heavy Fuel Oil (HFO). Our supply capabilities are backed by formal arrangements with major oil companies and trading partners, ensuring product quality and availability across all regions.

We have supplied over 50 million litres of diesel and gasoline to construction and infrastructure companies, including large-scale rail and civil works projects, and maintain the systems and fleet capacity to serve similarly demanding corporate requirements.
