EPC & Energy Infrastructure

End-to-end design, construction, and operation of petroleum depots, storage facilities and retail forecourts — from site selection and regulatory compliance to commissioning.

Overview

Infrastructure that underpins supply security

We engage in the design, construction and operation of petroleum depots, storage facilities and retail forecourts. Our infrastructure services extend from site selection and regulatory compliance to EPC implementation and commissioning.

Transaxis constructed and currently manages a diesel and gasoline tank farm with a total storage capacity of 360,000 litres, developed to support both our own supply operations and third-party storage needs.
